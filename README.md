📊 HR Attrition Analytics Dashboard – Power BI
🔎 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive HR analytics dashboard built using Power BI.
The objective is to identify key drivers of employee attrition and provide actionable insights for improving retention strategies.

The dashboard enables HR leaders and decision-makers to monitor attrition trends, income segmentation, job satisfaction, and departmental risk levels.

🎯 Business Objectives

Identify high-risk departments

Analyze attrition by age group

Study income band impact on attrition

Evaluate job satisfaction patterns

Understand promotion cycle influence

Support data-driven retention planning

📈 Key KPIs

Overtime Attrition Count

Overtime Attrition Rate %

Average Years Before Exit

Average Income of Leavers

Highest Department Attrition Rate

📊 Dashboard Insights

Attrition Trend by Age

Income Band Segmentation (Yes vs No)

Promotion Years vs Attrition

Satisfaction Heatmap by Job Role

Department Risk Ranking Table

Power BI (Data Modeling, DAX, Visualization)
